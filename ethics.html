Publication Ethics and Malpractice Statement

IJMCH follows ethical standards in accordance with COPE guidelines.

Peer Review Process

All submitted manuscripts undergo double-blind peer review by at least two independent reviewers.

Plagiarism Policy

All manuscripts are screened using plagiarism detection software. Manuscripts with more than 15% similarity may be rejected.

Authorship Criteria

Authorship should be based on substantial contributions to conception, design, analysis, and manuscript drafting.

Retraction Policy

Articles may be retracted in cases of ethical misconduct or serious errors.
