Aims & Scope

Indian Journal of Mental and Community Health (IJMCH) publishes high-quality original research, systematic reviews, case reports, and public health studies in psychiatry, psychology, mental health sciences, and community medicine. The journal aims to promote evidence-based practices improving mental health services and community healthcare outcomes.
