About the Journal

Indian Journal of Mental and Community Health (IJMCH) is a peer-reviewed, open access monthly journal based in India. The journal publishes original research, systematic reviews, case reports, and public health studies in psychiatry, psychology, mental health sciences, and community medicine.

Aims and Scope

Aims and Scope The Indian Journal of Mental and Community Health (IJMCH) is a peer-reviewed, open-access multidisciplinary journal dedicated to advancing knowledge, research, and practice in the fields of mental health, public health, and community medicine. The journal aims to provide a scientific platform for clinicians, researchers, academicians, public health professionals, psychologists, psychiatrists, social scientists, and healthcare policymakers to disseminate high-quality evidence that contributes to the promotion of mental well-being and improvement of community health outcomes. The journal publishes original research articles, systematic reviews, meta-analyses, narrative reviews, case reports, short communications, editorials, perspectives, letters to the editor, and public health policy papers. IJMCH encourages evidence-based research that addresses emerging health challenges at local, national, and global levels. The scope of the journal includes, but is not limited to: Psychiatry and clinical psychology Mental health promotion and prevention Community psychiatry and psychosocial rehabilitation Public health and community medicine Epidemiology and biostatistics Behavioral sciences and health education Substance use and addiction medicine Child and adolescent mental health Geriatric mental health Women’s mental health Occupational and environmental health Health systems, healthcare management, and policy Primary healthcare and family medicine Digital health, telemedicine, and artificial intelligence in healthcare Non-communicable and communicable diseases Social determinants of health Lifestyle medicine and preventive healthcare Rehabilitation sciences and quality of life research The journal particularly welcomes interdisciplinary studies that integrate mental health with community-based healthcare approaches and promote equitable, accessible, and sustainable healthcare practices. IJMCH seeks to foster scientific exchange, encourage innovation, and support evidence-informed decision-making for improving population health and mental well-being globally.

Publication Frequency

Monthly (12 issues per year)

Open Access Policy

This journal provides immediate open access to its content on the principle that making research freely available supports greater global knowledge exchange.
